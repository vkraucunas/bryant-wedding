About Us

Years before going on their first date, they had a professional relationship working in the airport area of Denver. In 2015 at a charity golf tournament, Charles asked for her number. She surprised him when she told him he already had it. To prove it, she typed in her number, called herself, and low and behold her name popped up. All this time he had her number. He asked her out that day and their first date was sushi.

When he bought a place in Colorado Springs, she helped him make it a home. She eventually found work in the Springs, and moved in with him. Finally, they added the Tank the wonder pup to the family.
